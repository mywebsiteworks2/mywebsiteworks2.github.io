Who we are

 Transmaju Ekspresindo was established in 1991 as a comprehensive overland cargo transportation company. They draw on the extensive facilities and professional resources of the parent company to provide rapid and reliable door to door cargo transportation

Transmaju services include :
1. Urban long-distance trucking
2. Brokerage
3. Customs clearance

Vision & Mission

 Vision : To become Indonesia's most efficient and reliable cargo transportation company.

Mission : To expedite in Indonesia's emerging market as a global trading partner by providing international standards of quality and service geared towards the requirements of all the customers.
